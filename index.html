6 Trading Desk Heuristics
01
Bifurcate every index
OMV +25.8% YTD vs Wienerberger −24.6% within the same ATX — a 50pt spread. Long upstream energy, short energy consumers is the classical pair expression.
02
Hungary sovereign is a discrete fat-tail
S&P review May 29. Downgrade below BBB- = forced selling of HGB → HUF weakens → energy imports costlier → CPI spirals. OTP at 41.2% BUX weight is the mechanical vehicle.
03
Poland's NBP cycle is the most rate-sensitive trade
Three cuts pencilled to 3.25%. Each month the shock persists delays them. PKO/Pekao/mBank = direct mechanicals. Orlen is the rare Polish long that profits from the same news.
04
Prague is the regional safe harbour
CPI 1.6%, clean fiscal, CNB credibility. CEZ nuclear = structural earnings recovery in H2 once current hedges roll off. PX outperforms on prolonged shock basis.
05
Watch Verbund vs. intervention risk
72% hydro at zero marginal cost. TTF >€45 mechanically lifts EBITDA 15–25% vs consensus. But EU gas price cap = Verbund upside ceiling. Key ATX pair: Long Verbund / Short voestalpine.
06
Fertilizer prices = leading food-CPI indicator
3–6 month lag. Prices already +2.4% in Jan 2026. If TTF stays elevated through planting season (Apr–May), food CPI hits Q3–Q4. PL & HU most exposed as cereal economies.
